The Story

60 years ago, there was a man named Jakusan who possessed a godly power named Blood Core. It became legend.

Eleven-year-old Yorudan lives in a world where extraordinary powers manifest within a person's very core. Blood Core, the rarest and most mysterious, has eluded his family for generations.
